## Copy direction used in this repo
- Lead with the actual service area and job types so visitors know the business fits their needs right away.
- Keep the tone direct, local, and practical.
- Use only public facts from the live presence: location, contact info, service categories, free estimates, and the public project gallery.
- Avoid claims about reviews, licensing, years in business, or certifications unless they are already public.
