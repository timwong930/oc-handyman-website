## Copy direction used in this repo
- Lead with the actual service area and job types so visitors know the business fits their needs right away.
- Keep the tone direct, local, and practical.
- Use only public facts from the live presence: location, contact info, service categories, and free estimates.
- Avoid claims about reviews, licensing, years in business, or certifications unless they are already public.

## Page structure
- Hero with location, service summary, and clear call-to-action buttons
- Service cards with plain-English descriptions
- Service area section
- FAQ for common buyer questions
- Contact section with phone, email, and public profile links
